Welcome to EcoFest!

Date: May 25th - May 27th

Location: Golden Gate Park

About EcoFest:

EcoFest is a three day festival dedicated to promoting environmental sustainability and eco-friendly living practices. join us for a weekend filled with inspiriing events, interactive workshops, and exciting activities aimed at raising awareness among college students about the effects of climate change and promoting affordable, everyday eco-friendly living practices.
