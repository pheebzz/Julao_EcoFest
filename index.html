EcoFest 2024

Welcome to EcoFest

Date: May 25th - May 27th

Location: Golden Gate Park

About EcoFest:

EcoFest is a three-day festival dedicated to promoting environmental sustainability and eco-friendly living practices. Join us for a weekend filled with inspiring events, interactive workshops, and exciting activities aimed at raising awareness among college students about the effects of climate change and promoting affordable, everyday eco-friendly living practices.

Why it's Important

  • Understanding carbon emissions is crucial for combating climate change.
  • Support sustainable practices to create a healthier environment
  • Proceeds support The Nature's Conservancy's efforts in carbon neutrality and sustainability.

What to Expect:

Day 1: Green Future Seminar
  • Keynote speakers
  • Interactive workshops
  • Engaging presentations
Day 2: Zero-Waste Market & Green Tech Expo
  • Explore affordable eco-friendly products
  • Learn about zero-waste living and ways to save
  • Discover the latest green technologies
Day 3: Carbon-Neutral Concert & Community Clean-Up
  • Enjoy musical performances powered by renewable energy
  • Join us for a community clean-up event
  • Make a tangible difference in your school or community

Merchandise:

Don't forget to check out our exclusive EcoFest merch! Choose from a range of sustainable items, including reusable water bottles, organic cotton tote bags, bamboo sunglasses, and eco-friendly buttons. Support environmental sustainability while spreading the word about EcoFest!
