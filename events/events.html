Time: 9:00AM - 5:00PM

Green Future Seminar

  • Kick off EcoFest with the "Green Future Seminar" featuring keynote speakers, workshops, and presentations on environmental sustainability and climate action

  • Learn more about the California-based organization and sponsor for EcoFest who have globally supported climate action and positive-change.

    Green Tech Expo

  • Discover the latest innovations in green technology and renewable solutions catered for independent college students at our "Green Tech Expo." Interact with industry professionals, and explore affordable everyday resources for a greener future.

  • The last event will be to join up for a meaningful community-clean up where any volunteer can participate in cleaning up local parks and beaches. This is to promote community practices to remove litter, plant more trees, and help keep habitats and the environment healthy for everyone.
